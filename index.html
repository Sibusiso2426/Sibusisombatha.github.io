About me

The short version of how I got here, and what I'm focused on now.

I'm a Data Analyst and AI & Data Science Engineer based in Johannesburg, with a foundation in Mathematical Sciences and hands-on experience across analytics, supply chain planning, and full-stack development. My work sits at the intersection of clean data pipelines, statistical rigor, and applications people can actually use.

I've moved from spreadsheets and BI dashboards into building complete systems — data warehouses, ETL pipelines, machine learning models, and the web applications that surface them. I care as much about how a model is deployed and monitored as how it's trained.

Outside of work, I'm usually deep in a side build, on a soccer pitch, or planning the next phase of a small farming venture I'm growing from the ground up.
